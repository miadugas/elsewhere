# Elsewhere — Impeccable Design Context

## Design Context

### Users

People weighing an **interstate move** — e.g. someone in Michigan eyeing another state — who want a straight answer to _"what salary do I need over there to keep my current life?"_ Used on a phone, often on the couch, during a real financial/life decision. Mobile-first. Secondary jobs: "how far does my money go?" and "where could I afford?" (one engine, three doorways; v1 ships the move doorway).

### Brand Personality

Three words: **cartographic, confident, plainspoken.** It feels like a well-made road atlas, not a fintech app. Trustworthy because the data is real (BEA + HUD government sources), warm because moving is emotional, never slick or salesy. Voice lives in headlines, eyebrows, and empty states; utility UI says exactly what it is ("Compare", "The Basket", "you'd need").

### Aesthetic Direction

**Place-flavored cartography — "the texture of moving."**

- **Chrome** (locked): muted map backdrop, floating bottom-sheet cards, pill bottom nav, a confident always-dark slab for the hero result number.
- **Identity** (locked): the From→To **route/arc** as the brand motif, **state silhouettes** as icons, an **atlas palette** (terrain green, water blue, highway-sign warm-orange, contour linework).
- **Typography direction:** _Interstate signage._ Display = **Overpass** (Highway-Gothic lineage, ties to the route-orange). Body = **Public Sans** (the US government design-system face — quietly reinforces the gov-data backbone). Numerics = **Overpass Mono** (tabular figures for the hero parity number + index ticks).
- **Intensity:** _Refined & confident_ — restraint and precision; type + route motif + the big number carry it. Atlas-flavored, not maximalist.
- **Theme:** both light + dark (auto/light/dark switch already built). Light is the primary context; the result slab + pill nav stay dark in both.
- **Anti-references:** generic pastel fintech, cyan-on-dark AI dashboards, glassmorphism, Inter/DM Sans monoculture.

### Design Principles

1. **The number is the moment.** The required-salary figure is the hero — tabular signage numerics, maximal contrast, share-ready. Everything else supports it.
2. **Cartographic, never decorative-for-its-own-sake.** Route lines, contour, state shapes earn their place by meaning (the move, the place). No ambient map texture as filler.
3. **Government-grade trust, road-trip warmth.** Public Sans + real data signal credibility; Overpass + warm paper + the route-orange keep it human.
4. **Refined restraint.** One signage display, one civic body, one mono. Tight 5-step type scale, 4pt spacing, 60-30-10 weight. Let whitespace and hierarchy do the work.
5. **Logic stays in engines.** All math in `src/engines/` + `useComparison`. Design touches presentation only; props/emits stay intact; `npm test` stays green.
