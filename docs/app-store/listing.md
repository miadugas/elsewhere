## App Name · max 30

**Elsewhere: Cost of Living** _(25)_

Alternate: `Elsewhere — Salary & Moving` _(27)_

> **Why qualified, not bare "Elsewhere":** App Store search (Jun 2026) shows no
> exact "Elsewhere" app, so Apple would allow the bare name — but the namespace
> is crowded (11+ apps start with "Elsewhere…") and two companies are named
> Elsewhere (Elsewhere Technologies Inc, ELSEWHERE.TO LTD), plus a near-twin
> "else.where: Expat Relocation." A qualified title puts the heaviest keyword
> in the title (top ASO lever) and separates us from the pile.
>
> The home-screen label (CFBundleDisplayName) stays **Elsewhere** — the store
> title and app name are allowed to differ.
>
> ⚠️ Not legal advice: with two "Elsewhere" companies already shipping apps, do
> a quick USPTO search for "Elsewhere" in software/finance classes before
> committing.

## Description · max 4000

```
Should you take the job in Austin? Move home to be near family? Elsewhere
answers the one question that actually decides it:

"If I move from here to there, what salary keeps my life the same?"

Enter your city, your destination, and your pay. Elsewhere shows the salary
you'd need to land in the same spot you're in now — then makes the number
real by pricing the move in everyday things: a month's rent, a tank of gas,
a slice of pizza, a week of groceries.

No spreadsheets. No vague "Austin is 8% cheaper." Just your number, and what
changes when you swap zip codes.

WHAT YOU GET
• Your parity salary — the exact pay that holds your standard of living
• The Basket — a localized everyday-price breakdown so the math feels real
• Cheaper or pricier, at a glance — and by how much
• Explore — see where your current pay stretches furthest across the country
• Share your number — send a clean result card to whoever's deciding with you

BUILT ON REAL DATA
Elsewhere runs on official Bureau of Economic Analysis Regional Price
Parities across 384 U.S. metro areas — the same cost-of-living data
economists use — refreshed with live rent figures. No guesses, no crowd-
sourced noise.

YOURS, AND PRIVATE
Elsewhere collects nothing. No account, no ads, no tracking. Your salary and
your cities never leave your phone. The whole calculator works offline once
loaded — check a number on the plane, in the basement apartment showing, or
anywhere with no signal.

Moving is one of the biggest financial decisions you'll make. Elsewhere makes
the number you're really deciding on impossible to miss.
```
