# Privacy Policy — Elsewhere

**Effective date:** June 23, 2026

## The short version

Elsewhere collects nothing about you. There is no account, no analytics, no
advertising, and no tracking of any kind. Your salary and the cities you
compare stay on your device and are never sent to us or anyone else.

## What stays on your device

Elsewhere saves a few things locally so the app remembers your work between
sessions:

- The salary you enter and the cities you compare
- Your light/dark theme preference
- The comparison you "pin" to the home-screen widget

This information is stored on your device — in the app's local storage and, for
the pinned comparison, in a private container shared only with the Elsewhere
home-screen widget. **None of it is transmitted off your device, and we never
see it.**

## What we don't collect

We do **not** collect, store, or have access to:

- Your name, email, or any account information (there are no accounts)
- Your salary, income, or the cities you look up
- Your location
- Your contacts, photos, or device identifiers
- Analytics, usage, or behavioral data

Elsewhere contains no advertising SDKs, no analytics SDKs, and no third-party
trackers.

## Network requests

To keep its cost-of-living figures current, Elsewhere downloads reference data
(metro price parities and typical rents) from `data.miacodes.com`. These are
read-only requests for public, non-personal data. **Your salary and city
selections are never included in these requests.**

As with any internet service, the server and its content-delivery network may
record standard technical request information — such as IP address, timestamp,
and app/browser user agent — strictly to operate, secure, and troubleshoot the
service. These logs are not used to identify you and are never linked to your
in-app activity.

The calculator itself works fully offline once the app has loaded; the network
is used only to refresh reference data when it's available.

## The home-screen widget

The Elsewhere widget reads the single comparison you choose to pin from a
private, on-device app-group container. This data never leaves your device.

## Children's privacy

Elsewhere is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect personal
information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we'll update the effective date above and post the
revised version at this URL. Material changes will be reflected in the app's
release notes.

## Contact

Questions about privacy in Elsewhere? Email **privacy@miacodes.com**.
